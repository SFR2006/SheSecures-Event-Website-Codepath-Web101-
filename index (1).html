She Secures🔒: Own Your Digital Power

Empowering Women to Conquer the Digital World!

About the Event

Welcome to the Digital Safety for Women 3-Day Event Hub! This event, taking place at the She Secures Headquarters in New York, is designed to empower and educate women about navigating the digital world safely. Key topics will include online privacy, safe social media practices, and how to recognize and avoid digital threats. You'll find essential information about the event schedule, guest speakers, panelists, workshops, and registration details here.

Join us as we work together to create a safer online environment for women everywhere!

What to Expect:

  • Day 1: Digital Safety 101: Building Your Cyber Shield
  • Day 2: Privacy Power: Mastering Your Digital Footprint
  • Day 3: Spot the Threat: Navigating the Digital Danger Zone

Event Schedule

Digital Safety 101: Building Your Cyber Shield

Kick off the event with a panel of experts sharing tips on how to protect your online security. Afterward, learn simple steps to secure your personal information, create strong passwords, and build a cyber shield to keep you safe in the digital world.

Privacy Power: Mastering Your Digital Footprint

Learn how to take control of your online presence. Through expert tips and easy steps, discover how to protect your personal data, manage what you share online, and keep your digital footprint secure.

Spot the Threat: Navigating the Digital Danger Zone

Learn how to recognize online threats like scams and malware. This session will teach you how to stay safe by spotting digital dangers and protecting your personal information in the online world.
